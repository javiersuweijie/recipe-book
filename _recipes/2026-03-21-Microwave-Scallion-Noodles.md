---
layout: recipe
title: Microwave Scallion Noodles
description: A quick, single-serving version of scallion oil noodles made in the microwave.
date: 2026-03-21
servings: 1
prep_time: 5 minutes
cook_time: 6 minutes
ingredients:
  - 3-5 stalks scallions
  - 1 bowl cooked noodles
  - 1-2 tbsp olive oil
  - "Seasoning":
    - 1 tsp soy sauce
    - 1 tsp oyster sauce
    - 0.5 tsp sugar
    - Sesame seeds
    - Chopped scallions (for garnish)
notes:
  - Warning: The bowl will be very hot after microwaving. Be careful when removing it.
  - Make sure to cover the bowl with a lid before microwaving to prevent splatters and ensure even heating.
---

1. Chop the scallions (cut into sections) and place them in a microwave-safe bowl. Add the olive oil.

2. Cover the bowl with a lid. Microwave on high for 6 minutes. *Note: The oil will be extremely hot.*

3. Remove carefully. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sugar to the hot scallion oil mixture.

4. Add the hot cooked noodles to the bowl and toss well to coat.

5. Finish by garnishing with extra chopped scallions and sesame seeds.
