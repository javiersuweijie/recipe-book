---
layout: recipe
title: Steamed Chicken with Scallion Ginger Sauce
description: Steamed chicken drumsticks with a savory scallion ginger sauce.
servings: 2-3
prep_time: 15 minutes
cook_time: 30 minutes
ingredients:
  - 4 chicken drumsticks
  - Red dates
  - Goji berries
  - 4 slices ginger
  - Spring onion whites
  - Salt
  - "Sauce":
    - Spring onion greens (chopped)
    - 2 tbsp grated ginger
    - 6 cloves garlic
    - 2 tbsp light soy
    - 1 tbsp sugar
    - 3 tbsp neutral oil
notes:
  - Optional: fried shallots for garnish.
---

1. Arrange chicken around an inverted bowl on a plate.

2. Season with salt, add ginger, red dates, goji & spring onion whites.

3. Steam 30 mins.

4. Sauce: Pour hot oil over scallions, garlic & ginger. Add soy + sugar.

5. Finish: Shred chicken. Mix some chicken juices into sauce. Pour over chicken and top with scallion sauce.

6. Serve with rice.
