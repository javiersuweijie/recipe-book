---
layout: recipe
title: High Protein Peanut Pasta
description: Creamy Thai-inspired peanut pasta with Greek yogurt, perfect for high-protein meal prep. Recipe from Lennard Yeong.
servings: 4
prep_time: 15 min
cook_time: 20 min
ingredients:
  - 200g Greek yogurt
  - 2 tablespoons smooth peanut butter
  - 1 tablespoon lime juice
  - 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  - 1 teaspoon palm sugar or brown sugar
  - 1 garlic clove, grated
  - 1 small chili, finely minced
  - 250g Brown Rice Fusilli (Naturel Organic)
  - Salt to taste
  - Pasta water (as needed to thin sauce)
  - Cooked protein or vegetables (see notes)
  - Roasted peanuts, for garnish
  - Thai basil, for garnish
notes:
  - Sauce: Stir together Greek yogurt, peanut butter, lime juice, fish sauce, palm sugar, grated garlic, and minced chili until smooth.
  - If sauce is too thick, add a splash of pasta water to thin it out.
  - Cook pasta in lightly salted water, drain, and toss directly into the sauce while still hot.
  - Protein options: air-fried kale, cherry tomatoes, chicken breast, edamame, diced bell peppers, asparagus, broccoli, cauliflower, or shrimp.
  - Great for meal prep — sauce holds up well in the fridge.
---

1. **Make the sauce**: Combine Greek yogurt, peanut butter, lime juice, fish sauce, palm sugar, grated garlic, and minced chili in a bowl. Stir until smooth.

2. **Cook the pasta**: Cook Brown Rice Fusilli in lightly salted water according to package directions. Drain, reserving some pasta water.

3. **Combine**: Toss the hot drained pasta directly into the sauce. Add a splash of pasta water if needed to reach your desired consistency. Season with salt to taste.

4. **Add toppings**: Fold in your choice of cooked protein and/or vegetables. Popular options include air-fried kale, cherry tomatoes, chicken breast, edamame, bell peppers, asparagus, broccoli, cauliflower, or shrimp.

5. **Serve**: Garnish with roasted peanuts and fresh Thai basil. Serve immediately or store for meal prep.
